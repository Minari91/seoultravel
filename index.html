Seoul Travel Guide.2023

Find some attractive tourist spots in Seoul

Coex Starfield Library
06164 513, Yeongdong-daero, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

Located in the center of COEX Mall, Starfield Library is an open public space where anyone can freely come to sit down, take a break, and immerse themselves in books along with other bibliophiles.

Yukjeon Sikdang - Main Store
16, Nangye-ro 30-gil, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul

The most popular Korean BBQ restaurant in Seoul. You must try!

Starbucks Kyungdong 1960
3F-4F, Gosanja-ro 36-gil, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul

Kyungdong 1960 is a community in South Korea where Starbucks has opened its fifth community store, which was redeveloped from a former cinema.
